General

On laptop speakers, turn on macOS Voice Isolation (menu bar → Control Center → Microphone Mode → Voice Isolation) for the strongest echo removal. Debrief already requests system echo cancellation; Voice Isolation adds the OS's stronger model. Speaker labels are editable in any transcript — click a speaker name to rename or reassign.
